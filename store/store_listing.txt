FULL DESCRIPTION:
🔧 SWAP. SOLDER. SHINE. 🔧

SparkSwap is a warm, vintage-styled circuit puzzle with a twist: the tiles are all in the WRONG places. Pick up a tile, swap it with a neighbor, and rebuild the copper trace from the battery to the lamp.

💡 HOW TO PLAY
• Tap a circuit tile to pick it up
• Tap an adjacent tile to swap them
• Rebuild the copper path from battery to lamp
• Light the lamp to win — fewer swaps means more stars!

✨ FEATURES
• 150 levels across Easy 5×5, Medium 6×6 and Hard 7×7 boards
• Unique swap-based gameplay — no rotating, pure spatial logic
• Gorgeous vintage copper-and-brass circuit board style
• Live power flow — watch the current creep closer with every swap
• Par system: beat the target swap count for 3 stars
• Calm ambient workshop soundtrack
• Haptic feedback
• 100% offline, no ads, no purchases, zero data collection

🧠 A DIFFERENT KIND OF CIRCUIT PUZZLE
Every tile is fixed in shape — only its position is wrong. Plan your swap chains carefully: one clever swap can fix two problems at once, and the best engineers always hit par.

Swap smart. Light the lamp. 💡
